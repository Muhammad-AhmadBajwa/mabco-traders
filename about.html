Established in Lahore

Our Journey & Legacy

Founded in the vibrant commercial hub of Lahore, MABCO Traders was established with a clear mission: to simplify the complex procurement landscape for businesses in Pakistan. Recognizing the challenges that shopkeepers and corporate buyers face when dealing with fragmented supply chains, we set out to build a comprehensive, one-stop wholesale distribution company.

Over the years, we have grown from a local supplier into a trusted general order partner known for integrity, efficiency, and premium product sourcing. Today, we proudly serve a diverse clientele, combining modern trade practices with deeply rooted Pakistani business values of mutual trust and respect.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower retailers and corporate enterprises across Pakistan by providing seamless access to high-quality products at competitive wholesale prices. We commit to delivering exceptional service, optimizing supply chains, and building long-term partnerships through transparency and operational excellence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading and most trusted general order supplier in Pakistan, recognized for transforming wholesale trading through innovation and reliability. We aim to set the gold standard for B2B procurement, driving growth for our partners and the wider commercial community.
